In this game you play as an introverted character. You don't have many friends and apart from your job at a local IT company, you spend most of your time gaming or working on computers.

One day, you find an old PC with a floppy disc in the atti of the house you grew up in. You plug the PC in and insert the floppy disc. The next events take place seconds after that.

You play as a character, but choices you make are of impact on the story and the characters' personality

This game is best experienced with headphones
